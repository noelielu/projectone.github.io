ingredients

Chinese wheat noodle, cucumber, green onion,
protein (ham, tofu, chicken, egg), sesame seeds

Sesame paste, soy sauce, rice vinegar

Boil and wash noodles, shave cucumber,
chop green onions, cook proteins and chop into
slivers. Arrange and cover in sesame sauce (mix
sesame paste, soy sauce, and rice vinegar).

*your dog will appreciate any meat/veg scraps*
